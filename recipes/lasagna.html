Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a comforting Italian dish with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. It's perfect for family dinners and gatherings.

The combination of savory ground beef, aromatic herbs, and perfectly cooked pasta creates an unforgettable meal that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic until meat is browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, and salt. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Layer 4 noodles, half the ricotta mixture, 1/3 of mozzarella, and 1/3 of meat sauce.
  8. Repeat layers, then top with remaining noodles, sauce, and mozzarella. Sprinkle with Parmesan.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake 25 more minutes.
  10. Let stand 15 minutes before serving.
