Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza recipe features a crispy, chewy crust topped with tangy tomato sauce and melted cheese. It's a fun weekend project that brings authentic pizzeria flavor to your kitchen.

Customize with your favorite toppings and enjoy fresh, hot pizza that's better than delivery!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water with sugar. Let stand for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Add yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Knead dough for 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place dough in greased bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour until doubled.
  5. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  6. Punch down dough and roll out on a floured surface to desired thickness.
  7. Transfer to a greased pizza pan or baking sheet.
  8. Spread pizza sauce over dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  9. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and add your favorite toppings.
  10. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  11. Let cool for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.
